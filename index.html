Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our concrete services in Marlborough, MA.

How long does a new concrete driveway take to cure?

Typically, you can walk on new concrete after 24 hours, but we recommend waiting at least 7 days before driving a standard vehicle on it to ensure maximum strength and durability.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all residential and commercial projects in Marlborough and surrounding areas. Just give us a call or send us a message!

What is stamped concrete, and is it durable?

Stamped concrete is concrete that is patterned or textured to resemble brick, slate, or stone. It is extremely durable and, when properly sealed, can last for decades while maintaining its beautiful appearance.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Minas Concrete is fully licensed and insured, ensuring that your property and our team are protected throughout the entire project duration.

Why is my old concrete cracking, and can it be fixed?

Concrete can crack due to soil movement, freeze-thaw cycles, or age. Depending on the severity, we can often repair and seal cracks to prevent further damage, or recommend a resurfacing solution.
